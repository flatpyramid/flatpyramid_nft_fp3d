What is
FlatPyramid?

FlatPyramid is an NFT marketplace offering an extensive 3D model library that includes thousands of products useful in a number of industries including Gaming, Metaverse, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Film and Special Effects, and other Interactive Media.

FlatPyramid provides a marketplace platform where 3d artists and designers leverage the skills of the 3d artist community while reducing their overall production time and costs by trading ready-made stock 3d models in interchangeable 3d file formats (digital assets).

FlatPyramid

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is FlatPyramid?

FlatPyramid specializes in the distribution of 3d models, other digital content, and interactive media. It is a crowd-sourced marketplace with stock assets for Gaming, Metaverse, NFTs, Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Advertising and Marketing, Animation, Film Special Effects, TV and Media, Architecture and Design, Product Development, and 3D Printing.

3D artists and designers generate revenue from creating and selling their stock 3d models to other artists on FlatPyramid's asset marketplace. FlatPyramid’s growing library of digital assets has tens of thousands of 3d models for purchase or for free.

What are 3D Models?

3D models are digital assets used as building blocks for NFTs, Games, the Metaverse and many other immersive experiences in many industries including: Gaming, Metaverse, NFTs, Augmented Reality (AR), and Virtual Reality (VR), Advertising and Marketing, Animation, Film Special Effects, TV and Media, Architecture and Design, Product Development, and 3D Printing.

What Does FlatPyramid Do?

Our mission is to revolutionize the way 3D Models are traded and distributed.

We promote, sell and license 3d models (digital 3d content) created by 3D artists in the gaming, NFT, metaverse, advertising and digital media industry worldwide. We strive to provide maximum returns to intellectual property owners like 3D artists and deliver quality products to our valued customers.

We provide customers with the assets (3D Models) so they can focus on other core areas of their production pipeline, thus cutting down their production time and costs.

What is the FP3D Token?

FP3D is FlatPyramid's utility token (digital token of cryptocurrency) that will power FlatPyramid’s marketplace for 3d models and related products.

All NFT transactions made on FlatPyramid will use FP3D. This token introduces blockchain technology into a 3d model marketplace, presenting a new system where each 3d model will be represented through an owner.

Initially, FP3D will be available on two separate blockchains as a standard utility token with the same tokenomics and distribution. Later in the development stage, a bridge will be developed to allow the interconversion of tokens on several blockchains. Also, later in the road map additional blockchains will be added.

How will I be able to Download 3d Models and Get NFTs?
  1. Go to (www.flatpyramid.com) and browse the different listings of 3d models available. Then, upon our NFT product launch, you will be able to select "Buy NFT of 3D Model" and add the NFT of the 3D model to your cart.
  2. Then, click on "Collect" to buy the NFT of the 3D Model (you get both the NFT as well as the 3d model download).
  3. On the modal "Choose Network", select your blockchain network (Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain).
  4. Then, proceed to select and connect your wallet, choose MetaMask wallet (for Ethereum) or TrustWallet (Binance Smart Chain).
  5. Select the cryptocurrency you want to pay with (USDC or FP3D), then click on "Approve" to send the transaction to your wallet for confirmation.
  6. Once you confirm your transaction on your wallet interface, click on "Pay" to submit your purchase. Your NFT will appear under your collectibles once the transaction is complete.
