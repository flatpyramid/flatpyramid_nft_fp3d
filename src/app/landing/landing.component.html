What is
FlatPyramid ?

FlatPyramid is an NFT marketplace offering an extensive 3D model library that includes thousands of products useful in a number of industries.

FlatPyramid helps 3d artist/3d modelers to save time and money by providing stock and custom 3d models in the appropriate 3d file formats (obj, max, maya, stl, dae, fbx, glTF, etc.) for use in 3D Modeling Applications, Virtual Reality Applications, Augmented Reality Applications, 3D Printing and Video & Mobile Games.

FlatPyramid
Token ($FP3D)

FP3D is a utility token available on 2 blockchains, Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It is the native token that powers the Flat Pyramid 3d models NFT marketplace.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Flat Pyramid?

Flat Pyramid is an organization that specializes in the distribution of 3d models and other digital content and interactive media. It is a crowd sourced marketplace for Augmented Reality 3d models, 3d Printing 3d models, Mobile ready 3d models and Online game ready 3d models.

Flat Pyramid is an intermediary platform where digital artists generate revenue from selling their 3d content. Flat Pyramid’s growing library has tens of thousands of products for purchase or for free.

What Does Flat Pyramid Do?

Our mission is to revolutionize the way 3D Models are distributed and used. We promote, sell and license 3d models (digital 3d content) created by individuals and corporations in the digital media industry worldwide. We strive to provide maximum returns to intellectual property owners and deliver quality products to our valued customers in a dynamic and stimulating environment.

How Do I Download 3d Models and Get NFTs?

You need to first will open the FlatPyramid website (www.flatpyramid.com) and browse from the different listings of 3d models. You will then need to select and then add the selected model to the cart. After opening the cart, you will choose the button: Buy As NFT. This button will allow you to download the 3d model and get it’s image as an NFT. You will be redirected to a screen where you will choose one of the 2 blockchain networks (Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain) you want to buy from. The pros and cons of each network will be written. You will be able to connect MetaMask wallet and TrustWallet.

FlatPyramid is an NFT marketplace for a huge database of 3d models. FlatPyramid platform allows: users to download 3d models as NFTs, 3d modelers to sell their 3d model(s) as NFTs, and consumers to apply for a specific 3d model they want.
