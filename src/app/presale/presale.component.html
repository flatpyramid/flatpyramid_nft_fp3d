How to Buy FP3D Token

01.

Create an account at binance.com, kraken.com, bitpanda.com, kukoin.com or an exchanger of your choice. Exchange FIAT (Dollar, Euro, GBP) for Ethereum ($ETH) or the Binance Coin ($BNB).

02.

Open your browser and go to metamask.io. Download the MetaMask extension and set up a wallet. For mobile. get MetaMask’s iOS or Android app.

03.

Transfer $ETH or $BNB to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g. Trust Wallet, Coinbase, or Binance), or buy directly within MetaMask.

FlatPyramid is an NFT marketplace for a huge database of 3d models. FlatPyramid platform allows: users to download 3d models as NFTs, 3d modelers to sell their 3d model(s) as NFTs, and consumers to apply for a specific 3d model they want.
