Sample NFT 3D Model Marketplace

Available at Launch
Buy NFT of 3D Model

Go to (www.flatpyramid.com) and select "Buy NFT of 3D Model" to add the NFT of the 3D model to your cart.

Collect the NFT

Then, click on "Collect" to buy the NFT of the 3D Model

Choose Blockchain Network

Select your blockchain network (Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain).

Connect/Select Wallet

Then, proceed to select and connect your MetaMask wallet (for Ethereum) or TrustWallet (Binance Smart Chain).

Select Cryptocurrency

Select the cryptocurrency you want to pay with (USDC or FP3D), then click on "Approve" to send transaction to your wallet.

Confirm on Wallet

Confirm your transaction on your wallet interface

Pay for NFT

Once approved, click on "Pay" and your purchase transaction will be submitted.

View your NFT

After your transaction is complete, your NFT will appear under your collectibles.

Key Features

  • Pay through Crypto or FIAT

Buy each 3d model through crypto (FP3D, stablecoins, major currencies) or FIAT currency

  • Buy through Multiple Blockchains

Flat Pyramid allows transactions on both Ethereum Blockchain and the Binance Smart Chain, more blockchains to follow

  • Mint NFTs on every purchase

Receive an image of the 3d model as an NFT to prove your ownership of each product

How to Buy FP3D Token

01.

Create an account at binance.com, kraken.com, bitpanda.com, kukoin.com or an exchanger of your choice. Exchange FIAT (Dollar, Euro, GBP) for Ethereum ($ETH) or the Binance Coin ($BNB).

02.

Open your browser and go to metamask.io. Download the MetaMask extension and set up a wallet. For mobile, get MetaMask’s iOS or Android app.

03.

Transfer $ETH or $BNB to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g. Trust Wallet, Coinbase, or Binance), or buy directly within MetaMask.
